My name is Dominique and living the simple life saved my life through God's light

I used to be lost. I had so many things that i thought that i loved, but the truth was i couldn't even appreciate any of it because there were too much.

But then light broke forth to what it is today

How i turned things around

I was on the verge of a breakdown when i lost what matters alot to me then God came through. I won't go through a lot of details but once i received the holy spirit everything became stress free, i found myself getting things without stress. indeed God is life.

Now I live the simple life

Now that i am living a simple life, things are so much better . I'm less stressed, enjoy life and work more, and i have more free rest to enjoy.

With how much it turned my life around. I felt i had no choice but to start sharing how i did it. and how others can benefit from the holy spirit living a simple life as well, which is why i started this site.
